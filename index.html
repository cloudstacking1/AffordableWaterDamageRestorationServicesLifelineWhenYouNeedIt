What is Water Damage Restoration?

Water damage restoration is the process of repairing and restoring a property that has suffered water damage. It involves several key steps, including water extraction, drying, dehumidification, cleaning, and repairs. The goal is to remove any moisture that could cause long-term damage and restore the property to its original condition.
